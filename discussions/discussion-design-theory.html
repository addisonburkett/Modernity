Design Theory

Understanding the geometric foundations of Modernity.

Geometric Foundations

Modernity’s shapes are derived from simple mathematical forms: circles, diamonds, hexagons, and arcs. These forms create a sense of structure and calm.

Color & Light

Soft gradients and glass‑like layers create a sense of depth without clutter. Light is used sparingly to guide attention.

Motion

Transitions are slow, deliberate, and purposeful — designed to feel like stepping between rooms rather than switching screens.
