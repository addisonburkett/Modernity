Project Aurora

A study in ambient dashboards and passive information flow.

Concept

Aurora explores the idea of a dashboard that doesn’t demand attention. It surfaces information gently, using color, motion, and subtle geometry.

Features

  • Ambient weather visualization
  • Soft‑motion time indicators
  • SVG‑based geometric animations

Future Plans

Aurora will evolve into a fully modular dashboard with customizable widgets and Pi‑optimized performance.
